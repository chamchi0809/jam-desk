# Runner sprites

The runner sprite frames in this directory (`cat_*.png`, `horse_*.png`,
`parrot_*.png`) are taken from
[RunCat365](https://github.com/runcat-dev/RunCat365)
(`RunCat365/resources/runners/`), licensed under the
[Apache License 2.0](https://github.com/runcat-dev/RunCat365/blob/main/LICENSE).

They are used as monochrome masks (tinted via CSS) to animate the coding-agent
status badge in terminal panel titles and on the minimap.
